What is Poshmark?

Poshmark is a social commerce marketplace that was founded in 2011 where people can buy and sell new or used clothing, shoes, and accessories.
Poshmark currently has over 50 million sellers otherwhise known as "stylists" that list over 100 million items a year.

Selling on Poshmark

Pros

  • No Listing Fees
    • Unlike Ebay, Poshmark charges their stylists zero listing fees whenever they post an item.
      This allows sellers to post as much as they want in order to increase their sales and ultimately bring more buyers to their page.
  • Large Market
    • Even though Poshmark is currently only useable in the United States, there is still a vast amount of buyers and sellers that keep the money flowing.
  • Shipping
    • Poshmark charges buyers a flat fee for shipping a provides a shipping label. All you have to do is pack the clothes in a Priority Mail box and drop off at the post office.
    • Becasue there is a flat shipping fee of $7.11, buyers are more likely to purchase more of your items in order to make the shipping cost worth the money.

Cons

  • Selling Fees
    • Selling fees on Poshmark are the highest amongst all of the eccomerce sites. Poshmark charges a 20% final selling fee for every itemt that sell.
    • This means that if you sell a $200 coat, Poshmark will takes $40 off the top. This is 8% more than what Ebay asks for.
  • Poshmark App
    • Poshmark has the most unappealing app amongst all of the commerce platforms.
      The app has a horrible layout that makes it very confusing to find what you are looking for.
      The dark burgandy and egg shell white color scheme on the app makes it displeasing to the eye.
  • The Need to be Active
    • In order to make continuous sales on Poshmark, sellers must remain active on the site.
      This includes liking other sellers items, sharing other sellers items, sending offers to potential buyers, and creating styles out of multiple litings.
    • This makes the need to create sales unbelievably tedious and ultimately turns your sidehustle into a second job.
  • Privacy
    • Many users have reported that they have been harassed through their listings by some less than reputable buyers.
      Users are able to upload a profile picture on their page and this often brings unwanted attention to their selling page.
